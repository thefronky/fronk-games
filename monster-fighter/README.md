# Monster Fighter — Curse of the Witch

A first-person 3D monster-slaying game built with Three.js. Billboard
sprites (all art generated with `gpt-image-1`) for monsters, trees, fires,
huts, and the magical sword.

## Story
An evil witch cursed the world with a single spell. The earth split open
and monsters clawed their way up from below. A lone swordsman — **Monster
Fighter** — comes to save the land, armed with a magical sword that holds
the last magic of the old world.

## How to play
Open `index.html` via a local web server (it loads ES modules + assets):

```bash
cd ~/Earth/Code/games/monster-fighter
python3 -m http.server 8770
# then open http://localhost:8770
```

- **Begin** → watch the skippable opening cutscene (the screen shatters
  into the fight). **Skip** is always available.
- Click **CLICK TO FIGHT** to lock the mouse.
- **WASD** move · **MOUSE** look · **SPACE** jump · **SHIFT** sprint · **ESC** pause
- **Left-click** — long sword *slash* (melee; monsters flinch + get knocked back)
- **Right-click** — *magic bolt* from the sword tip (costs 5 magic)
- **F** — *fire bomb* max attack: huge explosion, hits everything in range (costs 20 magic)
- **E** — use a treasure chest / trade with the merchant
- **M** — toggle music

### Choose your weapon
At the start (after the intro) you **pick a weapon**, each with a real playstyle:
- **Arcane Sword** — balanced rune blade, reliable bolts
- **Lightning Blade** — bolts **chain** between enemies
- **Frost Glaive** — bolts **freeze & slow** foes
- **War Hammer** — devastating melee + a ground **shockwave**

### Day / Night loop (timed)
- **NIGHT (~3 min)** — dark; you carry a **lantern**; an endless, *themed*
  stream of monsters rises and chases you (THE DEAD WALK → SWARM → AERIAL
  ASSAULT → GRAVE LEGION → THE GIANTS COME → CHAOS). Survive the clock.
- **DAY (~1 min)** — the sun rises, monsters flee, and a timer counts down while
  you run the map grabbing **treasure chests** (magic + gold) and **gems/coins**.
- The **sun & moon arc across the sky** (the world turns). Each dawn = **level up + full heal**.

### Max attack & upgrades
- **F** = **Fire Bomb** by default; buy **Storm Caller** from the merchant to turn
  it into **lightning from the sky**. Buy **Bigger Blast** for more radius.
- **Armor Plating** adds damage resistance so you're not glass.

### Economy & merchant
- Killing monsters and grabbing loot earns **gold**. The **merchant** lives in a
  **hut with a sign and only appears by day** — press **E** nearby to trade for
  transformative upgrades: new weapon elements, Storm Caller, armor, bigger
  blast, max HP/magic, weapon power.

### Map
- Press **TAB** for a top-down **map** — zones (forest, graveyard, ruins, caves,
  castle), chest/gold markers, monsters, the merchant, and your heading.

### Movement & verticality
- **Space** to jump and to **climb ladders**. Climb up to **tree-top platforms**
  that hold **royal chests**, a stone staircase to a battlement, ramps and
  jump-to platforms. Going high keeps ground monsters off you — but **flyers**
  (bats, harpies, gargoyles, wisps) and **climbing spiders** will still come.
- Explore a big world incl. a **castle you can walk into** and a **cave tunnel**
  connecting two caverns.

### Bars & fires
- **Health** + **Magic** bars. Magic regens slowly, and **very fast near any
  fire/brazier — which also heals your health**.

### The world
An expansive map with distinct zones: **dead forest**, **graveyard**
(mausoleum/crypt/tombs), **ancient ruins**, and an enclosed **cave**
(rock walls + ceiling, glowing crystals). Big moon + starry night sky / warm
sun by day, 3-plane volumetric trees & structures, upright billboard monsters
(incl. giant troll, golem, cyclops) with ground shadows. Collision is thin —
you brush past trees, only solid structures block you.

## Assets
All sprites are generated by `generate_assets.py` (transparent-PNG
billboards via gpt-image-1). Re-run it to regenerate; it skips files that
already exist so it never re-spends:

```bash
cd ~/Earth/Code && python3 games/monster-fighter/generate_assets.py
```

The game has colored-label fallbacks for any missing sprite, so it runs
even if generation is incomplete. Total generation cost: ~$2 (under the
$3 budget). gpt-image-1 caps transparent generations at ~5/min, so the
script paces itself serially.

## Dev note
`window.MF` exposes a small debug bridge (`forceStart`, `spawn`, `shoot`,
`slash`, `tick`, `state`) used for automated testing without pointer lock.
Harmless in normal play.
