HOW IT WORKS

THE JOB

• You talk. Pick what to ask — up to 5 questions. Ask more and it's harassment (a mark).

• Their answers cut both ways. A guilty look isn't proof. An innocent one isn't either.

• PULL GUN raises your revolver — it takes a beat to settle. SHOOT only hits when it's on target. Fire mid-draw and you put one in the dirt. Each shot kicks up — let it fall back before the next. Six rounds.

• Draw on an innocent → a mark. Shoot an innocent → prison. They shoot first → the graveyard.

• Sometimes they go quiet, say "never mind"… and reach. You won't always get a warning.
